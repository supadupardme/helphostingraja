    <h1 dir="ltr" style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-size: xx-large;"><strong>Import your Email Accounts and Forwarders into cPanel</strong></span></h1>
    <br/>
    <p dir="ltr"><span style="font-size: x-large;"><strong>What do you mean by Address importer in cpanel?</strong></span></p>
    <br/>
    <p dir="ltr">cPanel address importer is utilized to create multiple email address or forwarders in your account. cPanel address importers will support 2 types of data import, Comma-separated value sheet (.csv), or Excel Spreadsheet (.xls). You can additionally
        use address importer to import email forwarder lists.</p>
    <br/>
    <p dir="ltr"><a href="https://www.hostingraja.in/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">cPanel hosting</a> offers you with multiple options that make your tasks easier. It also can be versatile and can be used for a range of general functions from website
        administration to website security. Hosting your website using the best cPanel technology will certainly increase your productivity with the rise in your capabilities in customizing the settings of your website according to your requirements.</p>
    <br/>
    <p dir="ltr"><span style="font-size: x-large;"><strong>Steps to Import Email Accounts and Forwarders into cPanel:</strong></span></p>
    <br/>
    <p dir="ltr">Step-1: Login to cpanel</p>
    <p dir="ltr">Step-2: Click on the Address Importer under the Email section</p>
    <br/>
    <p dir="ltr"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://image.hostingraja.in/images/article/help/import-email-forwarders1.png" alt="Address Importer" width="362" height="247" border="0" /></p>
    <br/>
    <p dir="ltr">Step-3: Select the kind of spreadsheet you are going to import, Email Accounts or Forwarders.</p>
    <br/>
    <p dir="ltr"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://image.hostingraja.in/images/article/help/import-email-forwarders2.png" alt="address importer email account" width="460" height="432" border="0" /></p>
    <br/>
    <p dir="ltr">Step-4: You have to browse and select your Excel Spreadsheet or CSV file. In case, if you have selected XLS file, then it will only request to treat the first row as the column header, if yes, then select the checkbox. Or if you have selected CSV
        file, then it will request for the delimiter. In most of the cases, it is a Comma, select the one you have, and Click on Next button.</p>
    <br/>
    <p dir="ltr"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://image.hostingraja.in/images/article/help/import-email-forwarders3.png" alt="Address importer choose email files" width="371" height="462" border="0" /></p>
    <br/>
    <p dir="ltr">Step-5: On Import Email Accounts page:</p>
    <p dir="ltr">==&gt;Choose the header for each column.</p>
    <p dir="ltr">==&gt;Choose the <a href="https://www.hostingraja.in/domains/">domain name</a> in which you require to create the emails.</p>
    <p dir="ltr">==&gt;And click on Next button.</p>
    <br/>
    <div class="help-image-block"><img src="https://image.hostingraja.in/images/article/help/import-email-forwarders4-one.png" alt="import the Email addresses" width="352" height="251" /> <img src="https://image.hostingraja.in/images/article/help/import-email-forwarders4-two.png"
        alt="import the Email password" width="404" height="274" /></div>
    <br/>
    <p dir="ltr"><img style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;" src="https://image.hostingraja.in/images/article/help/import-email-forwarders5.png" alt="Address importer step2" width="376" height="277" border="0" /></p>
    <br/>
    <p dir="ltr">Step-6: In the last step, it will show you a review of what is going to be created.</p>
    <p dir="ltr">==&gt; Click on finish to Import the Email addresses.</p>
